Pirate Coquette

Pirate Coquette is a deliciously contradictory mix of hyper-feminine coquette sweetness and swashbuckling pirate rebellion. Massive satin bows, delicate lace, and frilly details clash beautifully with leather corsets, gold chains, tricorn hats, and rugged pirate elements. She is the ultimate seductive pirate doll — adorable yet dangerously confident, ruling the seas with playful charm and commanding presence. Think lace-up corsets worn over ruffled blouses, micro skirts with buckles and chains, and oversized bows paired with dramatic cleavage and bold red lips.
