Athletic Preppy

Saddle Stitched

Saddle Stitched is old money athletic femininity at its most effortless — the look of a woman who grew up on horseback and has never once had to think about it. The wardrobe is built from the stable outward: impeccably fitted breeches, tall leather boots, and crisp show shirts that transition from the ring to the clubhouse without missing a beat. Off the horse, the silhouette softens into tailored hacking jackets, pressed chinos, and cashmere knits worn with the sleeves pushed up. The palette is the countryside in autumn — deep navy, hunter green, warm camel, ivory, and cognac leather — punctuated by the occasional herringbone or subtle tartan. Accessories are restrained but always right: a single strand of pearls, a silk scarf knotted at the throat, a signet ring. The overall effect is confident without trying, polished without effort — the easy authority of someone who has always known exactly where she belongs.
