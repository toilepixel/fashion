Bunny Bombshell

Bunny Bombshell captures the iconic, ultra-glamorous Playboy Bunny aesthetic reimagined for maximum sex appeal and confidence. The signature look features a strapless satin corset bodysuit with bow tie, matching cuffs and collar, sheer black tights or fishnets, sky-high heels, and of course the iconic bunny ears and fluffy tail. Every detail emphasizes an hourglass figure, long legs, and playful yet powerful femininity. She is the ultimate confident, teasing seductress who owns her sexuality completely.
