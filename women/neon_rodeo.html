Western Futuristic

Neon Rodeo

Neon Rodeo is what happens when the wild west gets struck by lightning and likes it. Classic cowgirl silhouettes — fringe, yokes, pearl snaps, bolo ties, Stetsons — are cranked into full color overdrive: electric pink leather chaps over acid yellow cutoffs, rhinestone-drenched denim jackets in turquoise, neon orange fringe dancing off a micro skirt. The palette ditches dusty earth tones entirely in favor of hot pink, electric blue, lime green, blazing orange, and vivid purple, all glittering under open sky festival sun. Fabrics lean into embellishment — rhinestone-studded denim, metallic leather, sequined cotton, iridescent fringe — keeping everything tactile and joyful. The mood is unabashedly festive: sweaty, sun-baked, and unapologetically loud. This is the girl dancing barefoot in the front row at Stagecoach, Stetson tilted, fringe flying, absolutely radiating good-time energy.
