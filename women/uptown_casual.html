Uptown Casual

Uptown Casual is the weekend mode of a woman who has expensive taste and the restraint not to shout about it. The cashmere is real. The jeans are the right dark wash and cost more than they look. The sneakers are white and clean. Everything is polished without appearing formal — a beautifully cut blazer worn with dark jeans and loafers, a cashmere crewneck tucked into tailored trousers, a silk blouse that reads as easy but isn't. The palette is classic and quietly luxurious: camel, navy, cream, soft grey, chocolate brown, and deep burgundy, with the occasional clean pop of cobalt or forest green. Nothing is flashy. The logo, if there is one, is small. The quality of the fabric does the talking, and it doesn't need to raise its voice. She brunches, she shops at the farmers market, she looks terrific doing both.
