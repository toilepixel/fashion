Seasonal Preppy

Regatta Siren

Regatta Siren is the effortlessly commanding woman who owns the yacht club, the sailing regatta, and every sunset dockside cocktail hour. She blends classic nautical tailoring with sexy modern femininity: crisp white linens, tailored navy pieces that hug her figure, strategic stripes, gold hardware accents, and breezy silhouettes that move with the wind. Think fitted polo dresses, high-waisted shorts with sailor details, off-shoulder blouses, and statement espadrilles or deck shoes. Her look radiates sun-kissed confidence, quiet luxury, and playful power — the woman who captains her own boat and turns every head on the marina.
