Gothic Coquette

Gothic Coquette masterfully blends hyper-feminine doll-like sweetness with dark gothic romance. Delicate lace, massive satin bows, frills, and soft pastels collide with black velvet, dramatic corsetry, crosses, and moody Victorian mourning details. She is the adorable yet dangerously seductive gothic doll — innocent wide eyes paired with deep crimson lips, layered black lace over blush pink, and an aura of quiet power. The perfect balance of cute, melancholic, and powerfully feminine.
