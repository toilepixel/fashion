Upper East Side Princess

Upper East Side Princess is Blair Waldorf at her most lethal: old-money polish, impeccable tailoring, and the quiet certainty that she owns Manhattan. Headbands are crowns, pearls are armor, and every look is a calculated power move. This is ladylike glamour weaponizedelegant on the surface, ruthless underneath. She doesnt raise her voice; she just raises an eyebrow and the world rearranges itself accordingly.
