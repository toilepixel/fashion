Coquette Character

Constructioness Coquette

Constructioness Coquette takes the rugged construction worker aesthetic and transforms it into an irresistibly cute, hyper-feminine fantasy. Massive satin bows, lace trim, and pastel accents decorate practical workwear. She wears tiny cargo shorts with oversized bows, cropped safety vests over delicate bralettes, hard hats decorated with ribbons, and tool belts styled like fashion accessories. The contrast between tough work elements and soft coquette details creates an adorable yet powerfully confident look.
