Capitol Elite

Capitol Elite is the ultra-powerful, wildly extravagant wardrobe of the ruling class in a dystopian spectacle arena world. Think impossible silhouettes, surgical enhancements, and couture that screams wealth and control. Towering wigs, feathered epaulettes, and liquid-metallic fabrics cling to her commanding, athletic frame while she casually dictates the fate of nations with a coy smile or a single raised brow. The palette explodes with hot magenta, acid green, chrome, and void black under harsh arena spotlights and holographic billboards. Nothing is subtle — every seam, every accessory, every exaggerated detail asserts absolute dominance. She is in charge, always.
