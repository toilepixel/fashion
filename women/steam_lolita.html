Steam Lolita

Steam Lolita is the clockwork doll who pirouettes through brass-lined corridors and rooftop airship decks. She spins in body-framing layers of Victorian engineeringbell skirts puffed over striped petticoats, corsets laced with copper grommets over ruffled blouses, bolero jackets pinned with gear brooches. Every piece fuses lace with machinery: miniature top hats crowned with tiny propellers, platform boots with gear heels, parasols that double as telescopes. The palette is sepia bronze, ivory, bottle green, and tarnished goldrendered in brocade, leather, lace, and velvet. She is invention in miniature: curious, untouchable, and always one cog-turn away from flight.
