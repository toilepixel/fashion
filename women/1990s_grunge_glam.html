1990s Grunge Glam

grunge glam is **anti-glamour glamour** black silk slips over combat boots, plaid flannel tied at the waist, calvin briefs peeking out. Colors are muted: black, ivory, grunge plaid, smoke. Fabrics are raw: ripped denim, babydoll lace, heroin-chic chiffon. Accessories are quiet: choker, dark lipstick, doc martens. The woman is effortless and devastating, smoking in a Seattle alley or walking a white Calvin runway, too cool to care. This is 1990s sex appeal **Nirvana riff, Kate Moss stare, less is lethal**.
