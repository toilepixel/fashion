Autumn Vixen

autumn vixen is crisp air on warm skin oversized cable knits sliding off shoulders, suede micros barely covering thigh, plaid flannel tied at the waist. Colors are earth-fire: burnt orange, olive, rust, deep wine. Fabrics cocoon and tease: suede, wool, sheer tights. Accessories smolder: rust lipstick, suede boots, cider steam. The woman is brooding and cozy, fog on her breath, leaves in her hair, every layer begging to be peeled. This is autumn sex appeal **warm beneath the chill**.
