Fey Overlord

Fey Overlord is the iron-willed ruler of the hidden fae courts — beautiful, ancient, and merciless beneath a veneer of delicate whimsy. Iridescent butterfly wings shimmer behind razor-sharp crystal armor and flowing petal silk that barely contains her powerful, athletic physique. She uses coy giggles, playful winks, and feigned sweetness as weapons of mass manipulation while her court trembles in awe and fear. The palette is luminous emerald, moonlit silver, poisonous violet, and liquid gold, rendered in impossible living fabrics that shift and glow with her mood. Lighting is eternal twilight: soft bioluminescent flora, floating spores, dramatic backlit wings, and glowing mushroom circles. She dominates every scene with absolute authority — even her most disarming smile carries the weight of centuries of unchallenged rule.
