Neo Pop

Neo-Y2K K-Pop is maximum performance glamour straight out of a 2020/2025 music video or world-tour stage. Think Blackpinks Kill This Love outfits mixed with aespas virtual-reality concepts and ITZYs razor-sharp confidence. Everything is bold, shiny, color-saturated, and built for choreography: patent vinyl, holographic fabrics, chrome chains, cut-outs, logos, and reflective surfaces. The palette screams neon and metallics. Lighting is dramatic, colorful, and high-energy. The woman is a total idolfierce, polished, powerful, and always camera-ready.
