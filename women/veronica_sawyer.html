Veronica Sawyer

Veronica Sawyer is the ultimate smart, conflicted, and quietly powerful 1980s high-school anti-heroine. She blends preppy good-girl aesthetics with a darker, more confident and seductive edge as the story progresses. Think pleated skirts, crisp blouses, cardigans, and pearls mixed with a knowing gaze and subtle rebellion. She is intelligent, beautiful, and increasingly unafraid to wield her femininity and wit as weapons. The aesthetic highlights her beauty, inner strength, and growing confidence.
