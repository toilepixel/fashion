Old Money Elegance

Effortless, expensive, eternally classy femininity. Think Breakfast at Tiffanys little black dresses, Jackie Kennedy pillbox hats and tailored coats, quiet luxury that whispers wealth and impeccable breeding. Clean lines, luxurious fabrics, neutral-to-jewel palette with perfect accessories. She looks like she summers in Capri and has never been late in her life.
