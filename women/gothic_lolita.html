Gothic Lolita

Gothic Lolita is the porcelain doll who haunts the cathedral steps and the underground tea party in the same breath. She twirls in body-framing layers that marry innocence with obsidian edgebell skirts puffed over lace petticoats, corsets laced tight over ruffled blouses, velvet capes clasped at the throat with cameos. Every piece drips Victorian mourning glamour: miniature top hats, fingerless lace gloves, platform Mary Janes with ankle straps. The palette is midnight black, blood burgundy, ivory, and tarnished silverrendered in velvet, lace, taffeta, and brocade. She is tragedy in miniature: delicate, untouchable, and always one curtsy away from vanishing into the crypt.
