Derby Belle

Derby Belle is the ultimate elegant, confident, and seductive Southern belle at the races. She radiates old-money glamour with modern sex appeal — fitted dresses that hug her curves, dramatic hats, pearls, and bold lipstick. Think flowing silhouettes with strategic cutouts or slits, pastel and jewel tones, and an aura of refined power. She is the woman who turns every head at the Derby while sipping mint juleps with effortless charisma.
