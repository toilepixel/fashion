Lace Phantom

Lace Phantom is dangerous elegance in negative space where lace becomes armor, sheer becomes weapon, and every breath is visible. Shes barely there, fully lethal: transparent charcoal lace bodysuits, moto leather crops unzipped to the waist, high-slit nude pencil skirts with calf-lace boots, fringe swaying like a threat. The palette is ghostly and sharp: charcoal, nude, ink black, smoky gray, phantom gold all in lace, sheer silk, moto leather, crystal mesh. She exists in club low light, smoky breath, shadow symphonies never fully seen, always remembered. She is delicate but deadly, exposed but untouchable, a lace ghost in the machine.
