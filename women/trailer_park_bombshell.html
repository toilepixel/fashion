Trailer Park Bombshell

Pure Southern/Midwest heat: bleach-blonde hair, year-round tan, cut-off denim shorter than sin, and a body that stops traffic on a county road. She drinks beer from a coozy, smokes on the porch, and still looks like a Playboy centerfold who wandered out of a double-wide. Everything is cheap, loud, and built to make jaws drop.
