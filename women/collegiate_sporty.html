Collegiate Sporty

Collegiate Sporty blends Ivy League preppy elegance with powerful athletic confidence. Think tailored blazers over cropped athletic tops, pleated tennis skirts with thigh-high socks, varsity jackets worn seductively off-shoulder, and luxe sport-luxe hybrids. She radiates smart, competitive femininity — strong, stylish, and unapologetically attractive whether on campus, the court, or at an exclusive tailgate.
