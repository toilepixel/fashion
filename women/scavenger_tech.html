Scavenger Tech

A gritty, resourceful future where high-tech gear is salvaged and combined with rugged, hand-repaired garments. This is 'Wasteland Chic' meets 'Cyber-Guerilla.' Everything is layered, practical, and bears the marks of the environment—dust, grease, and sun-bleaching—while still featuring bits of glowing tech or reinforced armor.
