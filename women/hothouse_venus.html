Hothouse Venus

Hothouse Venus is the carnivorous bloom who unfurls in the moonlit greenhouse and devours the midnight botanist in one breath. She writhes in body-molding latex petals that open like living trapscorsets shaped as flytrap jaws over translucent vine bodysuits, skirts of layered latex leaves dripping nectar, bolero jackets sprouting thorn spines. Every garment fuses flora with fetish: pollen-dusted skin, garter vines that coil the thigh, platform heels rooted in glass soil. The palette is deep orchid, venom green, dew white, and blood sap redrendered in glossy latex, translucent vinyl, living moss, and silk vines. She is natures fatal seduction: lush, untouchable, and always one snap away from swallowing you whole.
