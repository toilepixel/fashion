Coquette Cute

Bubblegum Brat

Bubblegum Brat is the girl who showed up to the mall in a cloud of pink and absolutely knows every head turned. She's cute and she's smug about it — candy-colored Y2K silhouettes with just enough edge to keep you guessing. Low-rise micro skirts in bubblegum pink sit next to studded belts worn loose on the hip; a sweet knotted baby tee gets paired with chunky platform boots and fishnet anklets; a lilac plaid skirt shows up with a rhinestone choker and fingerless gloves. The palette is an assault of bubblegum, lilac, hot pink, electric blue, and chrome silver — black used sparingly as punctuation, never the base. Hardware is butterfly and rhinestone but worn with attitude rather than innocence. Tulle makes an appearance but it's layered over fishnets or clashing under a studded jacket. The mood is bright flash photography and bad parking lot lighting — this girl doesn't need golden hour. She already knows.
