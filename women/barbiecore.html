Barbiecore

Hyperfem Barbiecore is unapologetic, luxurious femininity turned all the way up. Its Paris Hilton in 2004, modern high-glam influencers, and the ultimate doll fantasy, but the woman wearing it is the one in total control. Everything is shiny, pink, tight, and extra: rhinestones, patent leather, glitter, micro minis, sky-high platforms, and flawless glamour makeup. The vibe is rich, playful, confident, and powerfully sexy on her own terms. Shes the main character, the CEO of looking this good, and she knows it.
