Character Fetish

Pulse & Heels

Pulse & Heels is the bubbly, confident, and irresistibly charismatic nurse who turns medical care into playful seduction. She wears crisp white uniforms with short fitted skirts, sheer stockings, high heels, and red accents that highlight her figure while radiating joyful energy. Every smile, every teasing check-up, and every sway of her hips is delivered with genuine happiness and magnetic charm. She uses her caring-yet-flirty personality to keep patients (and everyone else) completely under her spell — always professional on the surface, but delightfully naughty underneath. She is happy, outgoing, and utterly in control of the room.
