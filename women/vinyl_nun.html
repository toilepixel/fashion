Fabric Fetish Character

Vinyl Nun

Vinyl Nun is the renegade sister who trades stained glass for strobe lights and vows for vinyl. She glides through confessional booths turned DJ pulpits in body-molding patent silhouetteshabits sculpted from high-gloss PVC with strategic cross cutouts, wimples framing crimson lips, corsets laced with rosary chains over fishnet bodysuits. Every garment fuses sacrilege with couture: thigh-high patent boots, latex gloves ending in gold crucifixes, micro-skirts flashing garter rosaries. The palette is patent black, confessional crimson, holy gold, and ivory skinrendered in glossy PVC, latex, fishnet, and metallic foil. She is blasphemy in beat: commanding, untouchable, and always one drop away from absolution.
