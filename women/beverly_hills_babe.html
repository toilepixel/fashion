Beverly Hills Babe

Beverly Hills Babe is peak 1995 Clueless glamour updated for now: Cher Horowitz energy with better fabrics and slightly spicier cuts. Everything is perfectly matched, ultra-preppy, and just a little overdressed for the occasion. Plaid minis, cashmere sets, satin slips, knee-highs, and designer backpacks in a candy-colored palette. Shes the queen bee whos nice (but still knows she looks better than everyone). Confident, coordinated, and always camera-readythis girl doesnt follow trends, she sets them while quoting Jane Austen from the passenger seat of a white Jeep.
