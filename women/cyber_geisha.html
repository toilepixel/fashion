Sci-Fi Fabric

Cyber Geisha

Cyber Geisha is the luminous courtesan of a rain-slicked megacity--part ancient entertainer, part neural ghost. She glides through holographic tea houses in body-skimming silhouettes, her kimono sleeves slit to the shoulder, her obi cinched over a plunging bodysuit. Every garment fuses tradition with circuitry: corseted jackets with fiber-optic seams, asymmetric skirts flashing chrome lining, wrap tops tied with LED cord. The palette is neon magenta, electric cyan, obsidian, and liquid mercuryrendered in silk, latex, mesh, and metallic foil. She is ritual reborn in code: graceful, lethal, and always one encrypted glance from vanishing into the grid.
