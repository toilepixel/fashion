Coquette Old Money

Coquette Old Money is the ultimate fusion of inherited elegance and playful hyper-femininity. She wears quiet luxury with a flirtatious, bow-adorned twist — delicate lace camisoles peeking from tailored blazers, high-waisted pleated skirts with oversized satin bows, pearl strands layered with ribbon chokers, and soft cashmere cardigans over corset-inspired tops. The silhouette celebrates the female form with fitted waists, soft volume, and strategic skin, all executed in refined neutrals with pops of blush, powder blue, and cream. Every detail feels both expensive and adorably seductive, radiating confident, playful power that feels simultaneously innocent and irresistible.
