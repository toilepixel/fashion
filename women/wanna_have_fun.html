Wanna Have Fun

Wanna Have Fun (Cyndi Lauper 80s) is pure technicolor rebellion: thrift-store prom dresses, neon tutus, safety pins, and a smile that says I make the rules. Its punk-pop joy exploded into rainbow layersquirky, fearless, and 100 % herself. This is the girl who turned orange hair and mismatched earrings into a revolution. Girls (and everyone else) just wanna have funand look this good doing it.
