Sandy Greaser

Sandy Greaser is the bold, sexy transformation of the good girl into a confident, leather-clad vixen. She owns her sexuality with tight black leather, red lipstick, teased hair, and sultry confidence. The look mixes 1950s greaser edge with powerful feminine allure — skin-tight pants, off-the-shoulder tops, and an attitude that says she knows exactly what she wants. Playful yet dominant, innocent charm flipped into irresistible bad-girl energy.
