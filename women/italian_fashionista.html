Italian Fashionista

The woman who makes every sidewalk feel like a runway. Think off-duty supermodel in Positano, front-row at Milan Fashion Week, or riding a Vespa through Trastevere at golden hour. Perfectly tousled hair, sun-kissed skin, designer everything (or perfect dupes), and an attitude that says I woke up sexy and Italian. Colors are Mediterranean luxury: deep tans, bright whites, pops of red and gold.
