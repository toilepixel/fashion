Metro Preppy

Metro Preppy is the modern city sophisticate who blends preppy polish with sporty ease and seductive confidence. Tailored blazers, pleated tennis skirts, striped polos, and sheer button-ups are layered with strategic sheers, high slits, and body-skimming fits that highlight her figure and radiate approachable power. Think gold-buttoned jackets over lace bralettes, A-line skirts with movement, loafers or heels, and subtle athletic details that keep the look dynamic. She exudes intelligent beauty, effortless charisma, and quiet dominance — the woman who turns a campus stroll or boardroom meeting into a runway moment while staying undeniably sexy and in control.
