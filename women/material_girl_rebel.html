Material Girl Rebel

Material Girl Rebel is peak Madonna 1983-1995: the woman who turned crucifixes into sex symbols, wedding dresses into clubwear, and corporate suits into bondage statements. Lace gloves, cone bras, rosaries, leather, and tulle collide in a perfect storm of Catholic-kitsch rebellion. This is pop royalty that invented modern provocationsexy, sacred, and always one step ahead of everyone else.
