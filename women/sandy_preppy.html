Sandy Preppy

Sandy Preppy captures the adorable, coquettish 1950s good-girl charm of Sandy before her transformation. She radiates sweet innocence with a flirty edge — fitted cardigans, pleated skirts, peter pan collars, and pastel colors that perfectly highlight her figure and youthful beauty. Every detail feels polished, playful, and confidently feminine, like the ultimate girl-next-door who knows exactly how cute she is.
