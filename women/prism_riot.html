Prism Riot

Prism Riot is the undisputed sovereign of the living color dimension where every hue and pattern is in constant, joyful revolt. She channels the riot into breathtaking, glamorous looks: clashing neon geometrics layered over bold florals, metallic stripes colliding with polka dots, all cut into dramatic, curve-celebrating silhouettes that scream luxury and control. Voluminous shoulders, sky-high slits, cascading layers of shimmer and sparkle, and intentional print explosions create a walking fireworks display, yet nothing is accidental — every outrageous combination is calculated to dominate the eye and the environment. She wears her brightest, most playful smile like a crown, knowing that the riot bends to her will the moment she steps into frame. She is glamorous, highly colorful, fun, and outrageously in charge.
