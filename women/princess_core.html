Cute Fantasy

Princess Core

Princess Core is fairytale made flesh ballgowns that pool like moonlight, corsets laced with crystal, puff sleeves that swallow the arms. Colors are royal pastels: ivory, rose gold, pale pink. Fabrics float and shimmer tulle, satin, brocade every inch embroidered or beaded. Accessories are heirlooms: pearl crowns, glass slippers, diamond chokers. The woman is sovereign and soft, moving through candlelit palaces and starlit gardens with the weightless grace of storybook royalty. This is wearable once-upon-a-time.
