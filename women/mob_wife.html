Mob Wife

The Mob Wife aesthetic channels unapologetic late-2020s Italian-American luxury: think Essex County McMansion meets Staten Island penthouse. Silky animal prints, massive real fur coats, body-skimming silk and cashmere, sky-high heels, razor-sharp nails, blown-out hair, and diamonds that could pay off a loan shark. She is glamorous, dangerous, hyper-feminine, and always looks expensive. Colors are rich and saturated: deep black, burgundy, emerald, gold, with accents of cream and the occasional bold leopard. Every outfit screams "my husband may be in concrete but I'm in Couture."
