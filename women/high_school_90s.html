High School 90s

High School 90s captures the cute, flirty, and confidently feminine energy of a popular 1990s high school girl. She mixes preppy and casual cool with sexy touches — baby tees, high-waisted jeans, flannel shirts tied at the waist, and glossy lips. She is the girl everyone wants to be or date — approachable yet powerfully attractive, with a playful spark that lights up every room.
