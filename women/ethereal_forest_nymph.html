Ethereal Forest Nymph

A delicate and otherworldly aesthetic inspired by ancient woodlands and mythical spirits. The look focuses on weightless, flowing fabrics that mimic the movement of water or mist. Details are fine and intricate, favoring asymmetry and soft, desaturated tones that feel as though they are glowing from within. The atmosphere is quiet, sacred, and timeless.
