Rogue Jedi

Rogue Jedi is the independent, battle-hardened wardrobe of a powerful Force user operating outside any order — part wandering knight, part dangerous renegade. Weathered robes, tactical leather, and glowing kyber accents cling to her commanding, athletic frame as she bends the Force to her will with quiet authority. A coy half-smile or subtle head tilt can disarm enemies before she unleashes raw power. The palette is deep obsidian, glowing crimson, weathered brown, and kyber purple under dramatic starship lighting, desert dunes, or ancient temple glow. She answers to no one and dominates every frame she enters.
