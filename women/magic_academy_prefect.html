Magic Academy Prefect

Magic Academy Prefect captures the commanding, scholarly elegance of a top student authority figure in a legendary school of witchcraft and wizardry. Tailored robes, enchanted house crests, and subtly animated accessories cling to her powerful, athletic frame as she enforces rules — or bends them — with absolute confidence. She may tilt her head with a coy, knowing smile, but every gesture carries the weight of ancient magic and institutional power. The palette blends deep emerald, scarlet, royal blue, and antique gold in rich, flowing fabrics under candlelit halls, floating candles, and swirling spell light. She owns the castle corridors and every scene she enters.
