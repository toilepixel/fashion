Boho Seasonal

Weekend Market

Weekend Market is the aesthetic of the best Saturday morning — unhurried, sun-warmed, smelling faintly of coffee and cut flowers. She picks up heirloom tomatoes and fresh bread and somehow looks wonderful doing it, in a way that could not have been entirely accidental. The look is relaxed bohemian with enough structure to keep it from going fully shapeless: wide-leg linen, breezy printed dresses, woven totes, a vintage denim jacket thrown over a floral slip. The palette is earthy and warm with bursts of botanical color — terracotta, warm cream, dusty olive, ochre, soft coral, and washes of indigo. Fabrics are natural and lived-in: linen, cotton, light chambray, a little gauze. Nothing is ironed. Nothing needs to be. The overall effect is genuinely, unself-consciously pretty — and she bought the best peaches at the market because she got there early.
