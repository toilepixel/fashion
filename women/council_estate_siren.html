Council Estate Siren

Rough-round-the-edges but undeniably fit. Think late-night kebab-shop lights, fake tan glow, and designer dupes mixed with market-stall gold. She grew up on a council estate, still has the accent, but looks like she could pull a footballer or rob a postcode. Hair is big, make-up is sharp, attitude is lethal, legs for days.
