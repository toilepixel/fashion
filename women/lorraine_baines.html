Lorraine Baines

Lorraine Baines (1955) is the sweet, flirty, and powerfully feminine 1950s teenager who knows how to turn heads. She blends classic good-girl charm with teasing confidence — poodle skirts, tight sweaters, pearls, and red lipstick. Her look is cute yet seductive, radiating youthful beauty and playful power that makes boys weak in the knees. She is the ultimate 50s dream girl who enjoys the attention.
