1920s Flapper Fatale

Flapper Fatale is rebellion in rhinestones beaded fringe that swings with every hip shake, dropped waists barely covering silk stockings, art-deco patterns flashing under dim bar lights. Colors are black, gold, deep emerald, ruby. Fabrics move: silk, chiffon, velvet, crystal mesh. Accessories are weapons: feather headbands, cigarette holders, pearl ropes knotted low. The woman is dangerous and free, dancing on tables in speakeasies, gin on her breath, eyes daring you to follow. This is 1920s sex appeal -- loud, fast, and gone by dawn.
