Melrose Place

Melrose Place captures the glamorous, seductive, and slightly dangerous energy of the slightly older, more sophisticated 90s crowd. She exudes mature confidence with body-hugging dresses, power suits with sexy twists, sleek hair, and bold makeup. Think plunging necklines, tailored blazers worn with almost nothing underneath, high heels, and an aura of ambition mixed with irresistible sensuality. She is the woman who knows exactly what she wants and how to get it.
