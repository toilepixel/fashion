1960s Mod Minx

mod minx is youthquake in patent micro-minis barely legal, white go-go boots to the thigh, graphic blocks of neon pink and black. Colors are pop-art: white, mod orange, electric blue. Fabrics are future-shiny: PVC, vinyl, courreges wool. Accessories are bold: false lashes, mod sunglasses, daisy pendants. The woman is doll-eyed and dangerous, dancing in a cage under strobe, legs for days, daring you to keep up. This is 1960s sex appeal **Beatles scream, Twiggy stare, London swing**.
