Military Coquette

Military Coquette combines hyper-feminine coquette sweetness with sharp military tailoring and commanding details. Massive satin bows, delicate lace, and playful frills are paired with structured jackets, epaulets, harness straps, polished buttons, and combat-inspired elements. She is the adorable yet powerfully dominant soldier doll — cute pink bows contrasting against crisp khaki, black, and olive green uniforms. The aesthetic radiates confident, disciplined femininity with a teasing, authoritative edge.
