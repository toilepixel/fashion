Biker Coquette

Biker Coquette takes the ultra-cute hyper-feminine coquette aesthetic and slams it against rebellious biker culture. Massive satin bows, lace, and pastel frills contrast sharply with black leather, chrome hardware, studs, and motorcycle boots. She is the adorable yet dangerously powerful biker babe who looks like she could break hearts and bikes in equal measure. Think oversized pink bows on leather jackets, micro skirts with chain details, glossy latex mixed with delicate lace, and heavy makeup paired with an innocent smile.
