Transformed

Transformed channels the bold, rebellious, and irresistibly sexy energy of a girl who grew up around cars, tools, and danger. This aesthetic blends garage grit with high-glam femininity: cropped mechanic jumpsuits worn off the shoulders, tight oil-stained tank tops, low-rise jeans or leather pants, heavy boots, and plenty of exposed skin. Think Mikaela Banes — confident, slightly dangerous, mechanically skilled, and devastatingly attractive. Greasy hands, smudged eyeliner, and a knowing smirk complete the look. Metallic accents, chain details, and automotive-inspired elements mix with feminine curves and powerful attitude. She's the woman who can fix your engine and break your heart in the same afternoon.
