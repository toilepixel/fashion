Velvet Vixen

Vixen Velvet is the after-hours temptress who owns the penthouse elevator and the velvet-roped club in the same breath. She steps out in body-caressing silhouettes that blur the line between lingerie and couturecorsets laced over sheer blouses, slit pencil skirts flashing lace tops of thigh-high stockings, blazers worn as dresses with nothing underneath. Every piece drips modern opulence: plunging necklines, cinched waists, and sky-high slits, all engineered for movement and midnight mischief. The palette is scarlet, midnight plum, liquid black, and molten rose-goldrendered in silk, satin, lace, and patent leather. She is desire made wearable: confident, untouchable, and always one heartbeat away from disappearing into the night.
