Yacht Babe

The Yacht Babe aesthetic embodies sophisticated coastal luxury, reminiscent of the French Riviera. It is defined by effortless glamour, expensive fabrics, and a palette dominated by crisp whites, deep navies, gold accents, and sky blues. Outfits are designed for leisure on the waterthink breezy, tailored separates, smart knitwear, and elegant resort wear. The overall look is polished, sun-kissed, and undeniably exclusive.
