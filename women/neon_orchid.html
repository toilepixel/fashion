Neon Orchid

Neon Orchid is tropical heat turned nightclub voltage where jungle humidity meets mirrored excess. Shes glossy, electric, and untouchable: cut-out bandage dresses in hot magenta, liquid gold jumpsuits, sheer mesh maxis slit to the hip, rhinestone chains dripping over sun-kissed skin.The palette is vibrant and venomous: electric lime, fire opal, plasma violet, iridescent bronze all in shiny lycra, crushed satin, chainmail, wet-look crepe.She moves in neon-lit glows, humid steam, pulsing reflections never in daylight, never in silence. She is sultry but lethal, flirtatious but in control, a rare flower blooming under blacklight.
