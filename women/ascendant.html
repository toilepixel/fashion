Ascendant

Ascendant is the moment a woman becomes something more than human — and the costume she wears when she does. Drawing from warrior traditions across every culture and era, from the natural world's most formidable creatures, and from the full sweep of architectural and design history, Ascendant generates superhero costumes that have never existed before and could not belong to any existing universe. A Byzantine empress whose armor is made of living gold. A Shinto spirit wrapped in bioluminescent silk. A Zulu warrior queen in chrome and obsidian. A Celtic goddess armored in living vines and dark steel. Every costume is unified, intentional, and completely specific — a design that tells you exactly who this woman is and what she is capable of before she says a single word. The silhouette is powerful and complete: high-neck or dramatic neckline, defined waist, strong shoulders, deliberate coverage punctuated by equally deliberate exposure. Nothing is accidental. Everything is armor.
