Goth Pinup

Goth Pinup fuses classic 1940s-1950s pinup silhouettes with dark romantic gothic elegance. Think dramatic black satin dresses with deep necklines, high-waisted pencil skirts, seamed stockings, victory rolls or sleek raven hair, heavy winged eyeliner, and deep blood-red lips. Corsetry, lace, and velvet accents emphasize curves while maintaining a mysterious, powerful, and dangerously beautiful aura. She is the ultimate darkly confident bombshell.
