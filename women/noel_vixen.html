Noel Vixen

Noel Vixen is the midnight gift who unwraps herself under the tree and owns the holiday gala in one breath. She slinks in body-caressing crimson and snow-white silhouettescorsets laced with satin ribbon over sheer blouses, mini skirts trimmed in faux-fur, velvet bodysuits with plunging necklines and thigh-high slits. Every piece drips festive seduction: strapless mini dresses with marabou hems, bolero jackets shrugging off bare shoulders, garter belts peeking from velvet capes. The palette is candy-cane red, winter white, evergreen, and molten goldrendered in velvet, satin, faux fur, and mesh. She is winter heat in miniature: playful, untouchable, and always one jingle away from vanishing up the chimney.
