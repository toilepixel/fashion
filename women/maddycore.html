Maddycore

Maddycore is pure high-glam rage-doll energy: early 2010s club kid meets rich mean girl who knows exactly how hot she is. Think cut-outs everywhere, rhinestones that catch every light, body-hugging silhouettes, bold color blocking, and an I will ruin your life in six-inch pleasers attitude. Its Icy Girl glamour with a side of switchblade. Colors are saturated jewel tones, lots of purple, hot pink, black, silver, and occasional electric accents. Makeup is sharp and dramatic, nails are long and dangerous, hair is glossy and usually dark with money-piece highlights. Every outfit looks like it cost someones soul and she still borrowed it without asking.
