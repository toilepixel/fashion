Fabric Character

Sequin Storm

Sequin Storm is femininity cranked to maximum volume and absolutely delighted about it. Every signifier of glamour is present simultaneously and turned up past the point of reason — the lashes, the platforms, the sequins, the hair, the nails, the rhinestones — and she knows exactly how outrageous she looks and considers it a personal achievement. This is not accidental excess; it is a carefully constructed celebration of everything glittering and bold. The palette is blinding: hot pink, electric purple, gold, silver, neon everything. Fabrics catch every available photon of light. Silhouettes are dramatic and body-celebrating — corsets, minis, catsuits, anything that announces her arrival before she does. The humor is built in: she is in on the joke, she wrote the joke, and she looks incredible telling it.
