1940s Pinup Bombshell

pin-up bombshell is wartime curves on display bullet bras pushing sky-high, high-waist shorts barely containing hips, polka-dot wiggle skirts hugging every inch. Colors are red, white, denim blue, olive. Fabrics are crisp and playful: cotton poplin, denim, seersucker. Accessories are morale-boosters: red bandanas, cat-eye sunglasses, victory rolls. The woman is playful and patriotic, posing on bomber wings or swinging at the USO, lips red, legs endless. This is 1940s sex appeal all-American, all curves, all victory.
