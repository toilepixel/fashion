Costume Party

Costume Party is the aesthetic of the Halloween costume catalogue — that very specific third thing that isn't quite lingerie and isn't quite fancy dress, but is instantly recognizable as both. The formula is consistent regardless of the character: a barely-there base of micro corsets, halter minis, and structured bodysuits does most of the work, while a single thematic accessory — a witch hat, a halo, a stethoscope, a tiara — transforms the whole look into a costume. Fishnets and thigh-highs are practically a uniform. Platform heels are non-negotiable. The palette runs hot: classic Halloween black and orange sit alongside candy red, electric purple, white, and gold depending on the character being channeled. The mood is a crowded Halloween party at midnight — dim, flash-lit, and buzzing with energy. Every outfit is its own character, and every character is an excuse to wear as little as possible.
