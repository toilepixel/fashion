Princess Casual

PC Casual is princess core distilled for daylight the same tulle and satin, but cut for errands and espresso. Puff sleeves shrink to cardigans, ballgown skirts become pleated midis, corsets turn into delicate bustiers under blazers. Colors stay royal-soft: pale pink, mint, champagne. Fabrics are lighter organza, silk, lace trim on cotton. Accessories are subtle: pearl studs, satin ribbons in hair, crystal belts over jeans. The woman is still sovereign, just sipping iced latte on a sunlit sidewalk, skirt fluttering in the breeze. This is fairytale you wear to brunch.
