Naughty List

Naughty List is Christmas night with the lights turned low — the holiday's rich palette and sumptuous textures pulled into something sleek, grown-up, and undeniably sexy. Deep crimson velvet, forest green satin, and icy champagne sequins do the heavy lifting, while faux fur trim at the cuff or hem adds just enough festive punctuation without tipping into costume territory. The silhouettes are fitted and minimal — off-shoulder micro dresses, corset tops, thigh-high boots — the kind of thing you wear to a Christmas party knowing full well you're the most dangerous person in the room. The palette reaches beyond the expected: midnight blue and silver for a Christmas Eve sky, deep plum for something richly seasonal, frosted white for an icy editorial moment. Accessories are festive but wearable — bauble drop earrings, a jingle bell anklet, a Santa hat worn at a reckless angle. The mood swings between a candlelit Christmas party buzzing with champagne energy and the cold blue stillness of a snowy night outside a glowing window. Both are Naughty List. Both are perfect.
