Velvet Bunny

Velvet Bunny is the bubbly, confident, and irresistibly charismatic woman who has claimed the iconic bunny uniform as her personal power suit. She wears the satin leotard, fluffy tail, bunny ears, fishnets, and high heels with playful sophistication and deliberate sensuality. Every tail wiggle, every bright smile, and every teasing glance is calculated to captivate while she runs the room. She decides who gets the best service, who gets her attention, and who gets gently put in their place. Happy, outgoing, and completely in control, she turns the classic fantasy into her own sophisticated game of charm and dominance.
