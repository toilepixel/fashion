Gym Bunny Devil

Gym Bunny Devil is the ultimate fusion of hyper-feminine gym siren and literal succubus energy. She lives in mirror selfies, red LED gym lighting, and the smell of pre-workout. Tiny sports bras, second-skin leggings with pentagrams or flames, devil horn headbands, glossy lips, and abs that could cut glass. Colors are black, fiery red, hot pink, with neon accents and glossy vinyl touches. She's confident, teasing, powerful, and knows every guy (and girl) in the gym is watching.
