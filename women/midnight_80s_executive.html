Midnight 80s Executive

Midnight 80s Executive is the dark, seductive, and commanding side of 1980s power dressing. She rules the corporate world after hours in sleek black and deep jewel tones, with exaggerated shoulder pads, severely tailored silhouettes, sheer blouses, and dangerously high slits. Leather, latex accents, and metallic hardware add a sharp, fetish-tinged edge to classic power suits. She is the ultimate ice-queen executive — beautiful, untouchable, and sexually dominant.
