Coquette Doll

Coquette Doll is bedroom-eyed glamour in candy floss babydoll dresses, mile-high puff sleeves, and skirts that barely skim the thigh. Its hyper-feminine runway: tulle petticoats under patent mini skirts, satin bows cinching impossible waists, heart cutouts flashing skin. Colors are confectionery bubblegum, marshmallow, powder blue fabrics glossy and weightless. The woman is a living doll: seductive, playful, legs for days, moving through pastel dollhouses and sugar-spun gardens with slow-motion allure. This is coquette turned up to 11, still chic enough for a nightclub.
