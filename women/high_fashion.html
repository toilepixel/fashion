High Fashion

High Fashion is pure, untouchable glamour: razor-sharp tailoring, extreme silhouettes, and a cool, almost severe elegance. Think Helmut Newton photographs, 90s supermodels, and modern Rick Owens or Balenciaga runway energy translated into wearable (but barely) art pieces. Colors are restricted to an ultra-luxe monochrome and jewel-tone palette; everything is impeccably structured, dramatically proportioned, and photographed like a sculpture. The woman wearing it doesnt need to smile; she owns the frame simply by existing.
