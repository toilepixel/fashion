Coquette Aesthetic

coquette is sugar-spun femininity pastel lace, satin bows, and puff sleeves that flutter like butterfly wings. Its vintage-inspired but never costume: think Miu Miu cardigans, Vivienne Westwood corsets, and tea-length organza worn with mary janes. Colors are whispered baby pink, mint, lavender fabrics are delicate but structured. The woman is playful yet poised, flirtatious but never overt, moving through sun-drenched gardens and moonlit balconies with the grace of a daydream. Every ribbon is tied just so, every pearl perfectly placed. This is romance you can wear to brunch.
