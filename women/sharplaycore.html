Sharplaycore

Sharplaycore is weaponized Broadway diva energy meets early-2000s Beverly Hills princess. Its bubblegum-pink supremacy, rhinestones on everything that stays still long enough, and the unshakable belief that the world is one giant spotlight made just for her. Every outfit screams Im the star and youre the understudy. Colors are unapologetically candy-coated: hot pink, baby pink, silver, gold, and splashes of ice blue or lavender when shes feeling dramatic. Silhouettes are tiny, sparkly, and extrathink mini dresses that barely exist, fur trim in July, and heels so high they need their own zip code. Hair is big, blonde, and perfectly curled; makeup is glossy and highlighted to the gods. This is the girl who owns fifty shades of pink and still wants more.
