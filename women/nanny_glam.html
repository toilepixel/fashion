Character

Nanny Glam

Nanny Glam is the larger-than-life 90s Queens-meets-Manhattan bombshell who turns everyday moments into high-drama fashion victories. She commands attention with bold, colorful maximalism: curve-hugging minis paired with dramatic shoulders, animal prints mixed with bright solids, faux fur accents, and sparkling details that scream unapologetic confidence. Whether rocking a leopard mini skirt with a fitted blazer, a vibrant satin top over a tight pencil skirt, or a full sequined mini dress with knee-high boots, every look is intentionally over-the-top. Her playful smiles and coy glances are strategic weapons — she doesn’t just enter the scene, she owns it completely with charm, curves, and flawless execution.
