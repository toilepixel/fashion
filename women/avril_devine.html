Avril Devine

Avril Devine is the ultimate early-2000s pop-punk princess — rebellious, confident, and effortlessly sexy. She mixes skater girl attitude with cute feminine details: baggy or fitted pants, tank tops, hoodies, ties, and bold makeup. Her look radiates 'I don't care' cool while highlighting her beauty and curves. She is the girl who rocks the stage, flips off the rules, and still looks irresistibly hot doing it.
