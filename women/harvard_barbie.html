Harvard Barbie

Harvard Barbie is Elle Woods in her full Bend-and-Snap glory: hot-pink power suits, perfect blowouts, and a 4.0 GPA. Shes the ultimate proof that you can be girly, love fashion, and still destroy in the courtroom. Everything is coordinated to the last pearl button, the palette is candy-bright, and the vibe is Im cute, rich, and I just won the case. Feminine doesnt mean fragile it means unstoppable.
