Coastal Skipper

Coastal Skipper is effortless nautical luxury. A confident, sun-kissed man in perfectly tailored linens, crisp whites, navy tones, and premium deck shoes. Think modern yacht owner or seasoned captain on a Mediterranean escape — rolled sleeves, open collars, relaxed but expensive silhouettes that move beautifully with sea breeze. The look radiates quiet wealth, competence, and timeless masculine elegance.
