JD

JD is the ultimate dark, rebellious, and dangerously charismatic anti-hero. He combines brooding intensity with effortless cool in a 1980s greaser-meets-grunge style. Think black trench coat, boots, dark jeans or trousers, layered shirts, and a perpetual cigarette. His look is rugged yet stylish, exuding quiet power, danger, and magnetic sexuality. He is the bad boy who makes everyone nervous — and secretly thrilled.
