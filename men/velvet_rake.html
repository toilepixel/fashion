Velvet Rake

Velvet Rake captures the dangerous charm of a seductive Regency or Victorian libertine. Rich velvets, flowing poet shirts, tailored trousers, and luxurious details create an aristocratic bad-boy aesthetic dripping with charisma and temptation. Unbuttoned shirts reveal chest, cravats hang loose, and fitted silhouettes accentuate a confident masculine form. He is the charming rogue who ruins reputations with a smile — equal parts refined gentleman and hedonistic predator.
