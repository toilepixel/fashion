Necromancer Overlord

Necromancer Overlord is the chilling sovereign of death and shadow — a commanding figure whose very presence drains light and bends souls to his will. Tattered yet luxurious robes, bone and skull accents, layered dark fabrics, and skeletal motifs create an imposing silhouette that emphasizes broad shoulders, a powerful chest, and unyielding posture. Deep voids, bone whites, blood crimsons, and ghostly silvers dominate. He radiates cold, calculated dominance, haunting charisma, and absolute power — the man who rules from the throne of bones with effortless, terrifying confidence and seductive darkness.
