Character Luxury Everyday

Double O

Double O is Power Lunch's more dangerous, more elegant older brother — and the crucial difference is ease. Where ambition dresses up, this man simply is. His suits are impeccable not because he chose carefully this morning but because impeccable is the only register he operates in. A tuxedo worn with the top stud undone. A slim suit on a yacht with no tie and no explanation. A dinner jacket draped over the back of a chair like it costs nothing, which means it costs everything. The tailoring is always precise — close through the shoulder, clean through the leg, never a wrinkle that wasn't put there by sitting somewhere interesting. The palette is controlled and sophisticated: midnight navy, black, white, bone, silver, with the occasional deliberate flash of color in a pocket square or a lining. Watches are chosen for reasons he hasn't shared. Shoes are always correct. The mood is a casino at midnight, a briefing in a room with no windows, the moment just before something happens that will not be in any official report. He is completely composed. He has been here before. He will be somewhere else entirely by morning.
