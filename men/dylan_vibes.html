Dylan Vibes

Dylan Vibes captures the effortlessly cool, laid-back, and undeniably attractive energy of Dylan Marshall. This aesthetic is the ultimate hot surfer/skater guy next door — tall, toned, and effortlessly sexy with a charming, slightly goofy confidence. Think perfectly messy hair, fitted tees that show off his chest and arms, low-slung jeans or cargo pants, layered flannels, beanies, and casual pieces that still highlight his athletic build. He mixes California surf culture with street style: graphic tees, hoodies, chain necklaces, and worn-in sneakers. The overall look is relaxed yet magnetic — the kind of guy who can roll out of bed looking hot, make you laugh, and still turn heads wherever he goes. Approachable, playful, and quietly powerful in his own charming way.
