Pretty Boy

Pretty Boy is the ultimate pretty, submissive boy who loves being soft and delicate in clearly male clothing. Feminized tailoring on shirts, trousers, and vests creates an ultra-feminine silhouette on a male frame — soft pastel colors, lace trims on collars and cuffs, tiny bows, cropped hems that still read as boyish, and perfectly groomed features. Everything feels dainty, obedient, and teasingly weak. He radiates sweet vulnerability, eager-to-please energy, and blushing beta charm while never actually wearing women's garments.
