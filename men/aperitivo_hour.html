Aperitivo Hour

Aperitivo Hour is the Italian art of looking effortlessly correct at that golden window between 6 and 8pm — linen blazer slung just right, a light shirt open one button too many, leather loafers without socks, the whole thing telegraphing that he dressed in four minutes and somehow won. The palette is warm Mediterranean: terracotta, sand, ivory, warm navy, dusty sage, and sun-bleached caramel. Nothing is pressed to within an inch of its life, and nothing is sloppy. There is a specific Italian genius for the wrinkle that is intentional, the collar that is artfully undone, the trousers with the perfect break. The mood is golden hour at an outdoor table, aperol in hand, entirely at ease in his own skin. Nonchalant but never careless. Charming without effort. The most stylish man in any room, and the only one who doesn't know it.
